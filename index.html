Key Features

ProcureIt application aims to simplify and streamline the procurement process for organizations, allowing for efficient management of purchase requests, approvals, and vendor interactions.

Supplier Directory

Fully featured vendor directory. Track all your vendors from a single portal.

Purchase Orders

Fast purchase order creation.

Goods Receipts

Create Goods Received Notes against your Purchase Orders. Achieve end-to-end visibility of procurement transactions.

About Us

The vision for ProcureIt is a procurement process that fits with your organizational procedures, ensures compliance with approval processes, provide a clear flow of data for audit through a user friendly portal.

The inspiration from this project is drawn from the personal experience of the project team. By engaging clients in different IT delivery projects, a number of pain points were articulated by executive teams. The need for traceability in the procurement process was one of these resounding challenges. To tackle this, it is necessary to deliver an app that combines an intutive interface with a solution that addresses the different users engaged in organizational procurement workflows.
